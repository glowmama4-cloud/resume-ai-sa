What is ATS — and why does it matter for your CV?

  • ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Most big companies in SA (banks, retail chains, government departments) use software that automatically scans your CV before a human ever sees it.
  • If your CV uses fancy tables, columns, graphics, or unusual fonts — the ATS can't read it properly and you get rejected before anyone even looks at you.
  • This tool rewrites your CV in a clean, simple, ATS-friendly format so the system can read it — and you actually get called for an interview.
  • Simple layout + right keywords + clear achievements = you get through the ATS gate. ✅
